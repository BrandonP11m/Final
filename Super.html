what is a Supercars

A 'supercar' car is a type of car that is extremely fast, often beautiful, and likely expensive. It is the pinnacle of automotive art and design. A supercar is a factory-built, street-legal vehicle that is not heavily modified or potentially illegal. Supercars combine speed, handling, unique design, and rarity. They represent exclusivity and luxury.

what is considered a Supercars

A car is considered a supercar based on several factors, such as performance, design, price, and exclusivity. Supercars must be fast, usually with a top speed of over 200 mph, and must have a power-to-weight ratio that exceeds that of other sports cars. They must also have an aerodynamic design, be aggressively styled, and have a certain impracticality to them.
